# Career & Progression (Gamification)

## Concept
LWT gamifies professional growth to encourage consistency and accountability, but strictly avoids "gamey" aesthetics. The progression feels like a corporate career ladder.

## XP & Levels
- **Gaining XP**: Merging PRs (completing tasks), communicating well in standups, hitting sprint deadlines.
- **Losing XP**: Missing deadlines, pushing code that fails the AI QA review multiple times.
- **Levels**: Junior -> Mid-Level -> Senior -> Staff -> Principal.

## Unlocks
As users level up, they unlock the ability to join more prestigious simulated companies (e.g., transitioning from a messy startup MVP to a highly regulated enterprise FinTech firm) which carry stricter AI evaluation rubrics.
