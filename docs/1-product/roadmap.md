# Roadmap

## MVP 1.0 (Current Target)
The foundational version focused strictly on execution and AI project management.
- Authentication & Professional Onboarding
- Project Selection (AI SaaS, Healthcare, etc.)
- AI Project Manager & Task Generation
- Task Submission & AI Review System
- Core Dashboard (XP, Performance Tracking)
- Basic Portfolio Generation
- Dark & Light Theme Implementation

## Phase 2: Teams & Organizations
- Multiple AI Personas (Tech Lead, QA, UI Designer)
- Sprint Board (Kanban) with dependencies
- Workspace Management (Orgs & Teams)
- Deep Performance Evaluation & Rubrics

## Phase 3: Infrastructure
- Calendar & Scheduled AI Standups
- Push Notifications & Email Alerts
- Advanced Analytics (Burndown charts, velocity tracking)

## Phase 4: Advanced AI Mentorship
- Human-like AI Mentor (always available for career coaching)
- AI Recruiter (mock interviews based on sprint performance)
- Persistent AI memory across multiple projects and organizations

## Phase 5 & 6: Scale
- Billing integration (Stripe) and SaaS pricing tiers
- Enterprise and University portals for batch onboarding
- 3D Virtual Workspace integration
- Production Launch & SOC2 compliance audits
