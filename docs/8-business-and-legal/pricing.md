# Pricing Tiers

*(Note: Exact prices subject to change based on AI API costs)*

## 1. Free Tier (Intern)
- Access to 1 basic simulated organization (e.g., Simple Startup).
- Standard AI Models (faster, less deep reasoning).
- 1 Sprint active at a time.
- Basic public portfolio.

## 2. Pro Tier (Professional)
- Access to complex, regulated simulated organizations (FinTech, Healthcare).
- Advanced AI Models (Gemini 1.5 Pro) for extremely deep code analysis.
- AI Mentor chat access.
- Verified Premium Portfolio with AI Letter of Recommendation.

## 3. Enterprise / University
- Bulk seats.
- Custom organization generation (e.g., a university can generate a company that exactly matches their curriculum).
- Admin dashboard to track student execution velocity.
